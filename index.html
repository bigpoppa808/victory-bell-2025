Verified Elite Donors

USC Associates Football Picnic

1:30 - 4:30 PM Founders Park, USC Campus Members Only ($25K+ donors)
Who's There

USC's most loyal and enthusiastic leaders and supporters. Members have given $25,000-$300,000+ to USC. Includes executives, philanthropists, hospital board members, and successful entrepreneurs.

Why It Matters

This is the highest net worth pregame event. USC Associates is the university's premier academic giving society since 1959. Members fund student scholarships and faculty awards.

Access Strategy

Membership requires $25K-$300K+ in giving. If you don't qualify, find a USC Associates member to invite you as a guest. The conversations here are worth cultivating the relationship.

Post-Game High Value

Post-Game: Downtown LA / LA Live

~8:30 PM onwards LA Live / DTLA Varies
Where to Go

JW Marriott LA Live - Upscale hotel bar steps from Crypto.com Arena. Rock & Reilly's USC Village - Alumni after-party hub. Perch DTLA - Rooftop with executives and professionals.

Why It Matters

Post-game is when the real conversations happen. Donors, executives, and alumni unwind after the game. Less crowded, more intimate, better for deep networking.

Strategy

Pre-book a small group dinner (2-4 high-value contacts) at a nearby restaurant. Invite people you meet at tailgates to join for post-game drinks. Have a specific venue in mind to suggest.
